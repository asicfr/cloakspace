ABOUT CLOAKSPACE

Cloakspace is not your typical social media platform which logs all sorts of data about the user. We do not store any of your data and simultaneously provide a smooth experience. We tried to provide all sorts of features which can be exploited without storing information about the user. There are no hidden costs involved. This is a completely free platform where you can leave your thoughts and read some more without constantly worrying of being tracked or judged by someone.

We hope that you have an enriching experience as you scroll through the feeds. Do leave a suggestion or feedback so that we can improve and cater to your needs.

NOTE: Cloakspace is best viewed on devices having a screen width of more than 1000px, that is laptops and desktops. However, other devices pretty much provide a great experience too.
